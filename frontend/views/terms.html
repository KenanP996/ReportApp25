  1. Authorized users must hold a verified organization email address.
  2. Users agree to maintain accuracy of company reports and pickup records.
  3. Team Leads may grant and revoke membership based on operational needs.
  4. Managers retain the ability to approve reports, schedule pickups, and export compliance data.
  5. All data access will be logged for transparency and auditing.

The application is still under active development. By accessing the current static prototype you acknowledge that no real data is transmitted until the FlightPHP backend and MySQL database are provisioned in future milestones.
