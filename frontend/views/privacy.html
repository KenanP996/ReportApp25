Privacy Notice

ReportApp25 stores organization data necessary to coordinate electronics donation pickups, manage team membership, and track compliance. During upcoming milestones the backend will expose REST endpoints secured by JWT and role-based access controls.

Data We Plan to Collect

  • Account details (name, email, hashed passwords, role assignments).
  • Pickup logistics (locations, time windows, confirmed items).
  • Team membership and application history.
  • Activity logs for auditing and issue resolution.

The backend will rely on MySQL with PDO prepared statements. FlightPHP middleware will enforce JWT verification and scoped access to protect personally identifiable information. For Milestone 1 only static data is stored in the browser.
